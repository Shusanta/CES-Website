Center For Entreprenurship Studies

Text: The WSU Center for Entrepreneurial Studies promotes, supports, and facilitates entrepreneurial learning, innovative skill building and student venture creation. The WSU Center for Entrepreneurial Studies desires to bring visibility to WSU’s entrepreneurial endeavors and to positively impact its reputation through the support and facilitation of top-tier academic research, highly visible events, student success in external competitions, student venture launch and career placement in existing firms.


Introduction

The Center serves as a leader in WSU’s university-wide entrepreneurial initiatives, providing an epicenter for students to learn, create and launch ventures. Housed within the Carson College of Business on WSU’s Pullman Campus, the Center offers events, scholarship programs, clubs and organizations and a business plan competition as avenues for graduate and undergraduate students to pursue their entrepreneurial journey. The Center is an open collaboration resource for students and entrepreneurs from all WSU majors and academic programs and welcomes cross-disciplinary venture teams.
